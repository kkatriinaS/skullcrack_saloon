About The Skullcrack Saloon

Founded to fuse cinematic stunt-work and playful destruction, Skullcrack is built by stunt-coordinators and set designers to deliver a safe, unforgettable experience. We provide trained guides, protective gear, sanitization, and a variety of staged sets designed for dramatic effect — and great footage.

Why choose us

  • Safety-first approach and certified crew
  • Multiple themed sets and prop packages
  • Filming support and fast content delivery

We do NOT use real glass

All glass and dangerous props are stage-safe (composites, sugar glass, foam, etc.).
